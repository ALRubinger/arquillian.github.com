Web-based Chat

If you don't have an IRC client installed or available, you can join the #jbosstesting channel using the web-based client below. Simply enter a nikename, answer the CAPTCHA challenge, then click Connect. Note that public messages in the channel are logged to transcripts.jboss.org.
